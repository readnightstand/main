Privacy Policy

Effective May 21, 2026

Who we are

nightstand, LLC operates nightstand at readnightstand.com  —  a book recommendation service. Questions about this policy can be directed to hello@readnightstand.com.

What we collect

When you sign up for early access, we collect your email address. That’s it. We don’t collect your name, location, or any other personal information unless you choose to provide it.

How we use it

Your email address is used solely to contact you about nightstand  —  to let you know when the product launches, and to share updates we think you’d care about. We will not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third party for marketing purposes.

How we store it

Email addresses are stored with Formspree, an email form service. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policy.

Unsubscribing

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can also email hello@readnightstand.com at any time to have your address removed.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to how we handle your data, we’ll update this page and note the new effective date. For significant changes, we’ll send an email to the address on file.

Contact

Questions or requests regarding your data: hello@readnightstand.com
